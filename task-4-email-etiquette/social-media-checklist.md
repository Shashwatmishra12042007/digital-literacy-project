**The Do's**:

Do audit your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your content.

Do use platforms like LinkedIn to network with peers and industry professionals.

Do pause and think before you post or share anything online.

Do verify the accuracy of news or information before forwarding it to others.

Do report cyberbullying, harassment, or suspicious accounts immediately.

**The Don'ts**:

Don't share sensitive personal information (like your home address, phone number, or passwords) publicly.

Don't post negative comments about your university, professors, or peers.

Don't engage in heated "flame wars" or online arguments in public comment sections.

Don't post inappropriate photos that could harm your future employment prospects.

Don't click on suspicious links or accept friend requests from completely unknown profiles.
