### 📖 Project Overview
This repository contains all the deliverables for the **CSE0001 - Digital Literacy** course project. As a selected Student Digital Ambassador, the goal of this portfolio is to demonstrate practical knowledge of digital tools, professional online communication, safe internet practices, and foundational cybercrime awareness. 

All practical tasks and reflections are organized into their respective module folders below.

---

### 📂 Module Summaries & Deliverables

#### [Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic](./task-1-presentation/)
* **Focus:** Visual communication of digital literacy concepts, essential student tools, and safe internet practices.
* **Deliverable:** A single-page infographic designed to educate peers.

#### [Task 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio](./task-2-portfolio/)
* **Focus:** Establishing a professional online presence.
* **Deliverable:** Setup and documentation of foundational profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle.

* **Focus:** Hands-on practice with programming and cloud collaboration tools.

#### [Task 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide](./task-4-email-etiquette/)

* **Deliverables:** Drafts of professional emails (extension request and internship inquiry) and a Social Media Do's & Don'ts Checklist.
