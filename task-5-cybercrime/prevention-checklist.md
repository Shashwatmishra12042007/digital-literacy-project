Stay Safe Online: A Student's Checklist


Never share your UPI PIN: Remember that a UPI PIN is only required to send money, never to receive money.


Verify payment requests: Always double-check the recipient's name and the exact amount before approving any collect request on your UPI app.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Turn this on for all your social media, email, and banking accounts.

Use unique passwords: Do not use the same password for your university portal and your personal accounts. Use a password manager if needed.

Beware of urgent requests: Scammers use a false sense of urgency (like a blocked account or failed exam fee). Always pause and verify independently.

Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking: Never make financial transactions while connected to unsecured, free public Wi-Fi networks at cafes or airports.

Keep software updated: Regularly update your phone's operating system and apps to patch security vulnerabilities.


Report Incidents Immediately: If you are a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930
